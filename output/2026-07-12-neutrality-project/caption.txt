The most neutral AI in 2026? Grok — measured, not claimed. An independent benchmark scored 18 AIs on politics, and 97 of 108 scores leaned left. 👀

AI Receipts · Ep. 1

The Neutrality Project gave 18 AI chatbots a ~4,000-question political survey across six topics — climate, social values, national identity, religion, money, war & peace. Nearly every model leaned toward social change; Grok 4.5 landed closest to dead center, and Microsoft's Phi-4 was the most one-sided (it also dodged 1 in 4 questions).
