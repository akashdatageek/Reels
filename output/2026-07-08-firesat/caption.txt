Satellites can't see a wildfire smaller than a soccer field. That just changed. 🛰️🔥

The first 3 operational FireSat satellites reached orbit this week on SpaceX Transporter-17. They spot fires as small as 5×5 m — a classroom, from ~600 km up, through smoke and clouds — and Google AI confirms each ignition against ~1,000 past images of the same spot. The prototype already caught an Oregon fire other satellites missed. Goal: 50+ satellites scanning the whole planet every 20 minutes by the early 2030s.
