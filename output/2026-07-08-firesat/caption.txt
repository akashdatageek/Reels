Google just put a smoke detector in orbit 🛰️🔥

The first 3 operational FireSat satellites reached orbit this week on SpaceX Transporter-17. Their infrared sensors spot fires as small as 5×5 m — through smoke and clouds — and Google AI confirms ignitions by checking each image against ~1,000 past views of the same spot. The goal: 50+ satellites by the early 2030s, updating the whole planet every 20 minutes.
