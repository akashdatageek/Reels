Your school spends more on energy than on textbooks 📚⚡

US districts burn ~$8B a year on energy — and about 30% of it is wasted (ENERGY STAR/DOE). YC-backed Edviro puts AI agents on the problem: they connect meters, controls and utility data, watch every building 24/7, catch waste as it starts, simulate the fix, and prove the savings on the actual bill.

Source: Edviro (edviroenergy.com) · Sector stats: ENERGY STAR / US DOE
