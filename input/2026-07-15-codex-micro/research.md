## The spine

- **What:** Codex Micro — a $230 mini keyboard, co-designed by Work Louder,
  sold on OpenAI's own Supply Co. store. **OpenAI's first piece of hardware.**
- **Who:** OpenAI (ChatGPT) × Work Louder (specialty keyboard maker).
- **What it does:** physical controls for the Codex AI coder — joystick starts
  jobs, keys approve/reject, a dial sets how hard the AI thinks, six keys glow
  with each AI worker's live status.
- **Why it matters:** programmers are shifting from typing code to supervising
  AI workers — this is the first purpose-built desk console for that job.
- **Status:** limited run; already out of stock on the page.

## Honest caveats

- ⚠️ "First hardware product" = first *shipped* OpenAI-branded hardware (the
  io/Jony Ive device is unreleased); phrase as "first piece of hardware."
