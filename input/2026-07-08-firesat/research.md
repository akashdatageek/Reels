# Research — FireSat: first 3 operational wildfire satellites (2026-07-08)

## Story

The first three operational **FireSat** satellites reached orbit on July 7, 2026
(SpaceX Transporter-17 rideshare from Vandenberg SFB). FireSat is a
wildfire-detection constellation led by the nonprofit **Earth Fire Alliance**,
with satellites built by **Muon Space** and AI + detection tech from
**Google Research**. Marks the transition from the single Protoflight demo
(Transporter-13, March 2025) to an operational constellation.
