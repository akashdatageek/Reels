# Edviro — script development

## V1 (draft)
> HOOK: "AI agents are coming for school energy bills."
> → Edviro is YC-backed → connects meters, watches 24/7 → M&V reports → follow.

## Critique of V1
1. Hook is an announcement, not a stake — nobody cares about M&V reports.
2. The villain (waste of taxpayer money at staggering scale) never appears.
3. "$8B, more than textbooks" is the felt contrast — V1 buries it.
4. No receipt moment: the "catches waste as it starts → proves it on the bill"
   loop IS the receipt; make it a beat.
5. No callback in outro.

### V2 voiceover (~85 words ≈ 34s brisk)
1. "Your school probably spends more on energy than on textbooks and computers combined."
2. "US districts burn nearly eight billion dollars a year on energy — and about thirty percent of it is just wasted."
3. "A YC startup called Edviro puts AI agents on that problem. They connect the meters and controls, and watch every building around the clock."
4. "When something starts wasting power, the agent catches it, simulates the fix, and then proves the savings on the actual bill."
5. "Less waste, more textbook money. Follow for daily AI news."
