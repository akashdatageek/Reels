# Research — Edviro: agentic energy management for schools (2026-07-09)

## Story

Edviro (YC-backed) builds "agentic energy management for school districts":
AI that connects a district's meters, controls, and utility data, watches
every building 24/7, catches energy waste as it starts, fixes it, and proves
the savings with M&V reports.
