# Research — Brainbase "AI agent cloud" GA (2026-07-08)

## Story

Brainbase Labs (YC-backed startup, SF) launched general availability of
**Brainbase — "The AI agent cloud"**: a platform where you define an AI agent
in a few lines of YAML and push it to the cloud with one CLI command; the
platform handles hosting, scaling, and evaluations, across any model or harness.
Launch announced via a 4-video thread on @BrainbaseHQ (2026-07-07) and
"Brainbase is now GA" banner + announcement on brainbaselabs.com.
